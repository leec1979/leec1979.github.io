In the tradition of many comment sections around the Internet I must declare - First!

Now that's out of the way welcome to my blog, where you'll eventually read about my thoughts and adventures with technology, software and life as a British ex-pat in the US.

The first question many of you will have, especially those of a non-British persuasion, is "What is Marmite?". It is, quite simply, one of the greatest substances ever created by mankind. Especially when spread on warm, bready foodstuffs.

That leads us to the second question: "Why Marmite overlord?". That name was given to me many years ago by a friend (Hi Alan!) who, upon seeing just how much Marmite I was spreading on my toast, declared "You truly are the Marmite overlord". It became a great name to use online as it was never taken and so I fully embraced it.
